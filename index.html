Our Architecture & Meta Positioning

Full transparency on how SRLINES operates as a SaaS platform built on Meta's official WhatsApp Cloud API

Platform Architecture

SRLINES wCRM is a SaaS platform built directly on Meta's official WhatsApp Cloud API. We are not a BSP (Business Solution Provider) or a reseller. Our platform connects directly to Meta's Graph API endpoints, ensuring zero middleware latency and full API compliance.

  • Direct integration with Meta Graph API v21.0+
  • Node.js backend with enterprise-grade infrastructure
  • Real-time webhooks for message delivery & status
  • Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with 99.9% uptime

WABA Ownership

Each client owns their own WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) registered under their Meta Business Manager. SRLINES never owns, controls, or has admin access to client WABAs. Clients retain full ownership and portability of their phone numbers and message templates.

  • Client-owned WABA under their own Meta Business Manager
  • Full phone number portability & data ownership
  • Client-managed message templates & approvals
  • No vendor lock-in — migrate anytime

Billing & Payment Flow

All WhatsApp conversation charges are billed directly by Meta to the client's Meta Business Manager payment method. SRLINES only charges a SaaS platform subscription fee for access to wCRM features (CRM, chatbot, broadcast, integrations). There are no hidden markup fees on Meta's messaging costs.

  • Meta bills conversation charges directly to client
  • SRLINES charges only SaaS subscription fees
  • No markup on Meta's per-conversation pricing
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden costs

n8n & wCRM Integration

Connect WhatsApp to 400+ apps with visual workflow automation

Automate WhatsApp with n8n Workflows

n8n is a powerful open-source workflow automation platform that lets you connect any app to any app using visual, no-code workflows. Think of it as an open-source alternative to Zapier — but with full control and self-hosting options.

wCRM is an official n8n community node, published on npm and listed on the n8n creator marketplace. This means you can integrate WhatsApp Cloud API into any n8n workflow with just a few clicks.

  • Send WhatsApp messages from n8n workflows
  • Build chatbots with visual drag-and-drop workflows
  • Connect WhatsApp to 400+ apps (CRMs, databases, APIs)
  • Trigger automations on incoming WhatsApp messages
  • Free, open-source, and self-hostable
